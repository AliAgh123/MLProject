Diabetes Detection

A machine learning model designed to predict whether an individual is diabetic or not based on their medical data, such as glucose levels, blood pressure, and BMI. By analyzing these factors, the model provides valuable insights into the likelihood of diabetes, aiding in early diagnosis and proactive healthcare management.

What is Machine Learning?

Machine learning is a branch of artificial intelligence (AI) that enables computers to learn and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed. Instead of relying on predefined rules and instructions, machine learning algorithms analyze data, identify patterns, and make predictions or decisions.
